Welcome

Cargo Deductible Plus

At Cargo Deductible Plus, we help international shippers protect their bottom line when the unexpected happens. Freight claims are stressful enough — paying large insurance deductibles out of pocket shouldn’t add to the burden. Our program is designed to step in when claims occur, covering deductible costs so your cash flow stays strong and your operations keep moving.

We work with importers, exporters, freight forwarders, and logistics companies around the world. Whether your cargo moves by sea, air, or land, we provide a simple, reliable solution that bridges the financial gap between loss and recovery. Our plans are structured to be affordable, predictable, and easy to understand — no complicated fine print.

With years of experience in freight and risk management, our team understands the challenges international shippers face. That’s why we focus on fast response times, clear communication, and dependable support when you need it most. Our goal is to reduce financial risk, protect working capital, and give your business peace of mind with every shipment.

When cargo claims happen, we help you stay focused on running your business — not scrambling to cover deductibles. If you’re ready to lower your exposure and strengthen your shipping strategy, contact Cargo Deductible Plus today for a consultation. Let us help you move freight with confidence.
